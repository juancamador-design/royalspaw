Questions

Everything you need to know

What is mobile grooming and why is it better?

Mobile grooming brings a salon to your driveway. One-on-one attention, no other animals, no waiting room stress. Great for anxious, senior, or reactive dogs.

How do I know when the van is arriving?

You get an SMS confirmation, a 24-hour reminder, and a day-of message with a live location link when we’re on our way.

What is the “route flex” option?

When we’re already servicing dogs in your area, we can fit you in at 15% off. You give us a preferred week and we confirm the exact time within 24 hours.

Does my dog need vaccinations?

Yes. All dogs must be current on rabies at minimum. We ask you to confirm vaccination status in the booking form.

Can I watch while my dog is groomed?

For safety, we ask owners to wait inside. Sudden voices can startle dogs. We send you a photo when done!

Do you groom puppies?

Yes! Our Puppy Intro creates a positive first experience. We go at the puppy’s pace — no rushing, no stress.
