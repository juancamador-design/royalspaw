Questions

Everything you need to know 🐾

What is mobile grooming and why is it better?

Mobile grooming brings a fully equipped salon right to your driveway. Your dog gets one-on-one attention with no other animals, no cage, and no stressful waiting room. Especially wonderful for anxious, senior, or reactive dogs — they stay calm in familiar surroundings and get home faster. 🐾

How do I know when the van is arriving?

You'll receive an SMS confirmation when you book, a reminder 24 hours before, and a day-of message with a live location link when Alejandra is on her way. No more guessing!

What is the "route flex" option and how do I save 15%?

When we're already serving dogs in your neighborhood, we can fit you in at a 15% discount. You give us a preferred week and we confirm the exact time within 24 hours — usually within ±1 day. It's the best option if you're flexible and want to save! 🏷️

Does my dog need to be vaccinated?

Yes — all dogs must be current on their rabies vaccination at minimum, as required by Texas law. We ask you to confirm vaccine status in the booking form for the safety of your dog, our team, and all the other pets we serve.

Can I watch while my dog is being groomed?

We ask owners to wait inside during the appointment. Sudden voices or movement can startle dogs at inconvenient moments. Don't worry — we'll send you a beautiful photo when your pup is done! 📸💕

Do you groom puppies?

Absolutely! Our Puppy Intro is designed to make grooming a positive, stress-free first experience. We go at the puppy's pace — no rushing, no stress. Starting them right makes every future session so much easier. 🐶
